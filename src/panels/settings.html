Memory Settings
This is the memory of your game. Allocate more memory will increase the FPS. Be careful because if too much memory is allocated, it may decrease the performance of other apps on your computer.

- Maximum memory: Shows the total amount of RAM allocated to your game. It is recommended that you do not exceed 50% of the total amount of RAM on your PC in order for it to function properly.
If your computer has more than 8GB of RAM, we recommend that you allocate 5 if you have 8GB and 12 if you have 16GB. If your computer has 4GB or less, do not allocate more than 2GB.

- Minimal memory Shows the minimal quantity of RAM allocated to the startup of your game. It is recommended not to put more than 3GB for this value. The typical value is 1GB
